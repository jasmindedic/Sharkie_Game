THE SHARKIE ADVENTURE GAME

HOW TO PLAY

Sharkie just wants to swim quietly in the vast ocean. But there are many dangers.

Help Sharkie to dodge mean jellyfish and pufferfish who can deplete his lifepoints.

Collect at least 5 coins to be able to confront the final boss.

If Sharkie took damage, restore his health back with hearts scattered across the ocean.

Press the arrow keys to move Sharkie across the ocean.

Press the space bar to shoot a bubble at the endboss.
